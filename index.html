About

The Bus Finder website helps users easily find the correct platform number at major bus stations. It is especially useful for busy places like BMTC Bus station (Majestic) in Bengaluru. By entering the bus number or destination, users can quickly see the correct platform.This saves time and reduces stress, especially for new or elderly passengers. There is no need to download any app it works directly in the browser.The goal is to make public transport easier and more organized.Bus Finder brings clarity and convenience to every bus journey.
